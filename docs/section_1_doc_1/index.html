좋아하다/안 좋아하다

#Use

We can use ~좋아하다 or ~안 좋아하다 to discuss what we like or don't like. 좋아하다 means to like and 안 is the equivalent as don't. In korean we can omit, I in the sentence because its implied.

#Examples

  • 🐱
    고양이
    를
    👍
    좋아해
    요.
    I like cats.
  • 🐕
    개
    를
    👎
    안 좋아해
    요.
    I dislike dogs.

#Practice

  • 🐮 소 Cow
  • 🐴 말 Horse
  • 🐰 토끼 Rabbit
